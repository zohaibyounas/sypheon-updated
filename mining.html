Mining

There will be mining at Syphon Online.

To mine Sypheon Tokens players have to build Sypheon Miner Ship in the game. Once they built Miner ship they can Start mining of Sypheon Tokens by sending Sypheon Miner Ships to Blackholes.

To find blackholes, players should search galaxy and find the blackholes. Once they found the coordinates of Blackhole they can send their Sypheon Miner Ships to Earn Sypheon Tokens.

Every Black Hole has different rate of Sypheon Distirbution every minute. Sypheon Token distirbution from blackhole depends on amounth of Sypheon Miner Ships on that blackhole orbit.

Blackhole mining rewards will increase in line with game revenues. In other words, as new players join the game and the income of the game increases, the number of tokens per black hole mining and the number of black holes in the galaxy will increase. This increase is limited to 10%. The amount of tokens scattered from black holes varies depending on the size of the black hole. Each black hole begins to emit new radiation from scratch after 24 hours. In other words, the token amount is renewed. For example, if there are 50 tokens in a black hole and 25 have been collected in a 24-hour period, when the 24-hour period ends, the black hole glow is reset and it becomes 50 tokens again.
